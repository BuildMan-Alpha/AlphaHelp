Calendar Properties

Date format

Specify the format for dates.

Read only

Specify if the calendar allows data entry.

Switch year

If a date value has a two digit year, specify which year to switch from 1900 to 2000.

Use combo boxes

Specify if the calendar should show combo boxes for the year and month picker.

Low year

Specify the low year in the year picker.

High year

Specify the high year in the year picker.
