Sending and Receiving E-Mail

Alpha Five includes a system object for sending and receiving email.

Note : You may use the high level functions EMAIL_SEND(), EMAIL_SEND2(), and EMAIL_RECEIVE() instead of the low level Xbasic commands that deal directly with the "email" object. These functions are really just "wrappers" around the "email" object.
