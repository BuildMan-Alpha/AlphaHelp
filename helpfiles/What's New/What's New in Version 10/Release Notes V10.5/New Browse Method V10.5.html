<Browse>.command("current_to_top") Method

A new browse method allows you to scroll the record that currently has focus to be the first record in the browse.

Example:

Open the CustomerBrowse in AlphaSports and execute this command from the Interactive window:

CustomerBrowse.command("current_to_top")

Put a button with this code on the Invoice form in AlphaSports:

topparent:browse1.command("current_to_top")

Give focus to a row in the invoice_items embedded browse and click the button. The focus row will scroll to the top of the browse.
