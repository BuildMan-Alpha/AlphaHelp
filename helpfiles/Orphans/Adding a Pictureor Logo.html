Adding a Picture or Logo

One common letter design technique is to add a company or corporate logo to the top of the letter. If you have a bitmap image with your logo, you can add it by using the Bitmap tool on the Toolbox.

  1. To do this, click the Bitmap tool button .

  2. Click and drag over the area on which you want to place the graphic. (Graphics can only be placed outside of a Rich Text Object).

  3. When you release the mouse button, a blank bitmap object appears. To place your logo inside of the bitmap object, right click on it and choose Properties... from the right-click menu.

Supported By

Alpha Five Version 5 and Above
