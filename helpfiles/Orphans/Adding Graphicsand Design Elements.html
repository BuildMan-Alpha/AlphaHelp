Adding Graphics and Design Elements

You can make your Labels more visually appealing by adding lines, frames, pictures, and by adjusting the background. For example, if you are printing address Labels for customers, you can include your company logo next to the address on each Label.

Since these design objects are placed directly on the Label layout (not within a Rich Text object), you might need to first decrease the size of the Rich Text object. To do this, exit the Rich Text Editor (if necessary) by clicking outside of the Rich Text Object. Next, click once on the Rich Text Object to make the size handles appear. Click and drag any one of the size handles to adjust the object's size.
