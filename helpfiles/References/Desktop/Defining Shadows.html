Defining Shadows

You may apply shadows to many controls and layouts. To apply a shadow:

  1. Make a selection from the Style list. The options are:

    • "None"

    • "Thin"

    • "Medium"

    • "Thick"

    • "Thin Halftone"

    • "Medium Halftone"

    • "Thick Halftone"

    • "Thin Soft" - a gradient shadow

    • "Medium Soft" - a gradient shadow

    • "Thick Soft" - a gradient shadow

  2. Make a selection from the Color list.

Limitations

Desktop applications only.
