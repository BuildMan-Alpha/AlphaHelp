Adding Borders and Frames

Every object in the letter layout has border properties you may set. By default, most objects' borders are invisible. To make them appear, right click on the object, select Properties... > Border from the right-click menu, and set the border properties in the Properties dialog box.

If you want to create a border around two or more objects, use the Frame tool. To do this:

  1. Click the Frame tool on the Toolbox.

  2. Click and drag the mouse over the area on which you want to insert the frame.

  3. When you release the mouse button, the frame is created. To change the style, thickness, or color of the frame, right click on it and choose Properties... from the right-click menu. Refer to the paragraph above for more information on setting the frame's border properties.
