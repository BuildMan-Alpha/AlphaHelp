Defining Border Style and Color

Version 8 of Alpha Anywhere provides an expanded definition of border styles and color. You may apply these borders to controls and layouts.

  1. Make a selection from the Style picker. The options are:

    • "None

    • "Single line"

    • "Indented"

    • "Etched"

    • "Bump"

    • "Raised"

    • "Dots"

    • "Dashes"

    • "Long Dashes"

Line Style Picker

  1. If you did not select "None" in step 1, make a selection from the Edge Shape picker. The options are:

    • "Square"

    • "Oval"

    • "Small Rounded"

    • "Medium Rounded"

    • "Large Rounded"

    • "Small Beveled"

    • "Medium Beveled"

    • "Large Beveled"

Edge Shape Picker

  1. If you selected "Square" in the Edge Shape list:

    1. Optionally, make a selection from the line pixel Width list.

    2. Optionally, hide or show any of the four sides of the border.

Limitations

Desktop applications only.
