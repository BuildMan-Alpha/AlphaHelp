Using Frames with A5W Pages

You may use frames with A5W pages. However the Alpha Five HTML Editor does not support the creation or display of frames, so it is necessary to use a different editor such as  Dreamweaver or Textpad.

The pages within the frame construction can be either .HTM or .A5W format.
