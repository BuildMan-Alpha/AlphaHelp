Symmetrical Outer Join

Alpha Five allows you to create a symmetrical outer join with several steps.

  1. You join table A to B -> C, including all of table A's records.

  1. Then you join B to A -> D, including only the records where you have a matching link field.

  1. You subtract D from B -> E.

  1. Then you append C to E.
