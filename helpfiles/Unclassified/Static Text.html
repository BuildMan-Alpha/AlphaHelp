Static Text

In the previous examples, the text "Hello World" is referred to as "static text". Static text is comprised of any characters that don't have special meaning. For example, the '<' and '>' characters have a special meaning. as we have just seen, these characters indicate a button.

Other characters that have special meaning are: ? <> ', ? ()', ?[] ', ? {}' , ? | ' and ? ; ' . We will discuss the meaning of each of these characters later.
