How to suppress wiki search highlighting

After you have searched for a page in the wiki using full-text keywords, your keywords will be highlighted in the article in different colors. This is useful for finding specific topics, but annoying when you are trying to read the article.

To redisplay the page without the highlights, click on the name of the article in the location bar at the top of the page. For example, click on How to suppress wiki search highlighting in the string
::Location : Alphapedia > How to suppress wiki search highlighting::
at the top of this page to redisplay the page without highlights.
